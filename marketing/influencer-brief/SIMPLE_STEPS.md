## What If You Don't Have Analytics?

**No problem!** Just use your eyes:

1. **Look at the numbers:**
   - Videos with more likes/comments are usually better
   - If a video has 10K views and 500 likes, that's good
   - If a video has 10K views and 50 likes, maybe skip it

2. **Check the comments:**
   - Positive comments = good sign
   - People asking questions = engagement
   - Negative or controversial = skip it

3. **Trust your gut:**
   - If a video feels right, it probably is
   - If it represents your brand well, include it
   - If you'd want to show it to a new creator, it's a good example
